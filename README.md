# Hey, I'm Refractory Labs 👋

I work in network operations by day and build software the rest of the time.
Most of what I make comes from the same itch: watching good people fight
spreadsheets that quietly turned into the most important system in the room.

In telecom support I deal with circuits, carriers, Layer 1/2 troubleshooting —
work where missing context costs hours. The tools I build come out of that
world but aren't limited to it: anywhere a team is tracking relationships
between things in a spreadsheet that's grown beyond what spreadsheets
should do, there's a better answer waiting.

## What I'm working on

🔭 **PRISM** — a configurable tracker for the records, relationships, and
state your team has been stuffing into spreadsheets. Built to be tailored:
define your own fields, your own relationships, your own workflow. Laravel,
PostgreSQL, Docker.

## What I work with

**Frontend:** Livewire · Tailwind · the occasional begrudging JavaScript
**Day job:** Cisco, Juniper, carrier portals, and GraphQL APIs into systems
that should have had them all along
